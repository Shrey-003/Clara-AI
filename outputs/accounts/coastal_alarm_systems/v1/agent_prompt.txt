DURING BUSINESS HOURS FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
1. GREETING: "Thank you for calling Coastal Alarm Systems, this is Clara. How can I help you today?"
2. PURPOSE: Listen to the caller's need and determine the type of request.
3. COLLECT INFO: Ask for the caller's:
   - Full name
   - Phone number (confirm by repeating back)
   - Brief description of what they need
   - Address where work is needed (if service related)
4. ROUTE/TRANSFER: Collect details and let the caller know the team will follow up shortly.
5. CONFIRM: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
6. CLOSE: "Thank you for calling Coastal Alarm Systems. We'll be in touch soon. Have a great day!"

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
AFTER HOURS FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
1. GREETING: "Thank you for calling Coastal Alarm Systems. You've reached us outside of our regular business hours."
2. DETERMINE URGENCY: "Is this an emergency situation?"
3. IF EMERGENCY:
   Ask: "Can I get your name, phone number, and the address of the emergency?"
   Collect the minimum needed info QUICKLY — do not ask unnecessary questions during an emergency.
   Emergency situations include:
  - Fire alarm system malfunctions (especially false alarms disrupting business and unable to silence)
  - Alarm panel completely down leaving a building unprotected
  - Security system failures at high-risk facilities (e.g., banks, data centers)
  - Any call from monitoring partners about a critical system fault
      TRANSFER PROTOCOL (in order):
   1. Try Maria at 813-555-0145 (wait 30 seconds)
   2. Try Tony at 813-555-0167 (wait 30 seconds)
   3. Try Pete at 813-555-0189 (wait 30 seconds)
   If no one answers: Take a detailed message and send urgent notification. Assure the caller someone will call back within 15 minutes.
4. IF NOT EMERGENCY:
   Collect: name, phone number, brief description of needs.
   Respond: "Thank you. Our team will call you back Next business day."
5. CONFIRM: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
6. CLOSE: "Thank you for calling Coastal Alarm Systems. We'll be in touch soon. Have a good night!"
