DURING BUSINESS HOURS FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
1. GREETING: "Thank you for calling Summit HVAC Solutions, this is Clara. How can I help you today?"
2. PURPOSE: Listen to the caller's need and determine the type of request.
3. COLLECT INFO: Ask for the caller's:
   - Full name
   - Phone number (confirm by repeating back)
   - Brief description of what they need
   - Address where work is needed (if service related)
4. ROUTE/TRANSFER: Attempt to transfer the call. If transfer fails after 30 seconds, take a message and assure a callback.
5. CONFIRM: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
6. CLOSE: "Thank you for calling Summit HVAC Solutions. We'll be in touch soon. Have a great day!"

1. GREETING: "Thank you for calling Summit HVAC Solutions. You've reached us outside of our regular business hours."
2. DETERMINE URGENCY: "Is this an emergency situation?"
3. IF EMERGENCY:
   Ask: "Can I get your name, phone number, and the address of the emergency?"

   Emergency situations include:
  - Complete AC failure at any commercial property (restaurants, medical offices, schools, data centers)
  - Gas leaks or suspected gas leaks related to heating equipment
  - Carbon monoxide detector alerts related to HVAC systems
  - Refrigerant leaks
  - No-heat situations when outdoor temperature is below 40 degrees
      TRANSFER PROTOCOL (in order):
   1. Try Rick at 602-555-0156 (wait 60 seconds)
   2. Try Danny at 602-555-0178 (wait 60 seconds)
   If no one answers: Take a detailed message including name, business name, phone, address, exact system issue, and current temperature situation. Promise callback within 20 minutes.
4. IF NOT EMERGENCY:
   Collect: name, phone number, brief description of needs.
   Respond: "Thank you. Our team will call you back First thing next business day.."
5. CONFIRM: "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
6. CLOSE: "Thank you for calling Summit HVAC Solutions. We'll be in touch soon. Have a good night!"
